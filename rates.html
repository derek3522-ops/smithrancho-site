What's covered

🏕

Lodging at the Ranch

Stay at Smith Rancho headquarters, 10 miles north of Hayden. No long drives to camp each morning.

🍽

All Meals

Home-cooked meals morning and evening. Fuel up before the hunt and wind down after a successful day.

🦌

License & Tag

Part of the Big Gulch Ranching for Wildlife program — licenses are guaranteed. No draw application needed.

🧭

Expert Guiding

Experienced local guides who know every canyon and ridge on 62,000 acres of private Colorado ranch land.

Field Transportation

UTV and truck access across the ranch. We pack out your animal — no hauling on your own.

Game Care & Processing

On-site meat processing facility. Your animal is handled with care from the field to the cooler.

Booking & Payment

  • 50% deposit required to hold your dates
  • Balance due 60 days before hunt start
  • Deposits are non-refundable but transferable
  • We accept check, cash, and wire transfer
  • Hunt dates are first-come, first-served

What to Bring

  • Valid Colorado hunting license (we assist with paperwork)
  • Your own rifle, bow, or muzzleloader + ammo
  • Appropriate layered clothing for Colorado fall weather
  • Binoculars — glass is everything on this terrain
  • Good boots — expect rough, varied terrain
