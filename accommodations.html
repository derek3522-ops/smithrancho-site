The Lodge

Comfort at the end of every hunt

The lodge sits right on the ranch, surrounded by the same country you'll hunt all day. Come back each evening to a warm room, a hot shower, and a home-cooked meal — no driving back to town, no roughing it in a tent.

The great room is the heart of it: vaulted wood ceilings, an antler chandelier, and walls lined with decades of trophies taken right here. It's where hunters gather after dark to swap stories and plan the next morning.

Whether you stay in the main lodge or the log cabin, the goal is the same — rest well, eat well, and wake up ready for another day on 62,000 acres of Colorado high country.

Rest · Private Guest Rooms

Sleep like you earned it

Comfortable private rooms with real beds and quiet nights — the kind of rest you need before a 4:30 wake-up. Warm, simple, and welcoming, with that classic lodge feel throughout.

You'll spend your days covering hard country. The lodge is where you recover for the next one.

Gather · Kitchen & Dining

Home-cooked, every day

A full modern kitchen and a table big enough for the whole camp. Hearty breakfasts before first light, lunches packed for the field, and full dinners waiting when you come off the mountain.

Nobody goes hungry at Smith Rancho — good food is part of the hunt.

The Cabin · Log Retreat

The log cabin

A classic hand-built log cabin rounds out the lodging — log walls, a cozy lounge, and that timeless Western feel. A great option for a smaller group or anyone who wants a little extra quiet.

Lit up at dusk against the Colorado sky, it's about as good as a hunting basecamp gets.
